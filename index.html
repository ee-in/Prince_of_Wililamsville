About the Prince of Williamsville

A destination rich with timeless elegance, the historic Prince of Williamsville combines Troy-Del grandeur with contemporary suburban lux. Located in the heart of Buffalo's most charming suburb, "The Nawab"" offers an unparalleled experience of sophistication and comfort, where every detail reflects our commitment to that Royal treatment, but not in an offensively British way or anything.

Founded in the last century by our ancestors but actually us who are still alive, the Prince of Williamsville was established to serve as a welcoming stop for famliy travelers and friends in the region. Built with care and craftsmanship typical of the era, the inn quickly became a centerpiece of the community ‚Äî a place where stories were shared, deals were struck, and friendships began. Its distinctive architecture and warm hospitality reflected the values of those who built it: resilience, generosity, and pride in their town. Over the decades, the Prince of Williamsville has stood as a lasting symbol of heritage and connection for all who visit.
